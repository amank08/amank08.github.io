Site Map:

Page Design:

  • Homepage
    • Purpose: A landing page for users and also to display the mission of the brand
    • Audience Potential customers and visitors to the website.
    • Content: Introduction to the brand, mission statement, advertise new or featured products, and links to other sections.
    • Links: Navigation links to other pages on the site as well as links to my client's other pages.
    • Data Fields: None
    • Validations: None
  • Posters and Invitations
    • Purpose: To showcase the client's posters and invitations products.
    • Audience: Customers interested in purchasing posters and invitations.
    • Content: Images and descriptions of available posters and invitations, pricing information, and links to purchase.
    • Links: Navigation links to other pages. Shopping Cart button.
    • Data Fields: None
    • Validations: None
  • Journals & Notebooks
    • Purpose: To display the client's range of journals and notebooks.
    • Audience: Customers looking for high-quality journals and notebooks.
    • Content: Images and descriptions of available journals and notebooks, customization options, pricing information, and links to purchase.
    • Links: Navigation links to other pages. Shopping Cart button.
    • Data Fields: None
    • Validations: None
  • Planners
    • Purpose: To present the client's planner products.
    • Audience: Customers in need of planners for personal or professional use.
    • Content: Images and descriptions of available planners, customization options, pricing information, and links to purchase.
    • Links: Navigation links to other pages. Shopping Cart button.
    • Data Fields: None
    • Validations: None
  • Inquiries
    • Purpose: To allow users to submit inquiries or requests regarding the client's products.
    • Audience: Users with questions or specific requests.
    • Content: Contact form with fields for name, email, inquiry/message, and submit button.
    • Links: Navigation links to other pages. Shopping Cart button.
    • Data Fields: Name, Email, Phone, Questions
    • Validations: Email and Phone have to be in the required format
  • Shopping Cart
    • Purpose: Allow users to buy the items listed in their cart
    • Audience: Users that are looking to purchase our products
    • Content:
    • Links: Navigation links to other pages. Shopping Cart button.
    • Data Fields: Name, Email, Phone, Address, Credit Card Info
    • Validations: Email Phone and Credit Card Info have to be in the required format.

Dynamic Functionality:

  • Interactive Product Gallery: Allow users to hover over or click on product images to view additional details or enlarge the image. This will be present on Planners, Journals & Notebooks, and Posters & Invitations pages
    • Examples: Papier, Etsy
  • Shopping Cart: Enable users to add products to a virtual shopping cart and proceed to checkout seamlessly, enhancing the e-commerce functionality of the website.
    • Examples: H&M, Hollister
  • Customization Options: Incorporate interactive elements that allow users to customize certain products, such as journals or planners, by choosing colors, patterns, or adding personalized text.
    • Examples: Amazon, dbrand
